We Are The Problem

It goes without saying that littering the world has negative effects. The results of inappropriate waste management and trash disposal can lead to detrimental results of soil, ocean, air and groundwater pollution. It directly affects ecosystems and species health; including our own. It is only expected to get worse. Waste production is predicted to increase by 70% in the next 30 years unless major and rapid changes were to be made.
